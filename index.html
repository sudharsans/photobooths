Professional DSLR Camera and Lighting

Expect no blurry or grainy webcam photos here! Selfie Booth™ has a professional DSLR camera and studio strobe lighting to produce images with unmatched quality and resolution.

Sharing Is Caring

Selfie Booth™ shares your pictures like a champ! Guests can interact through a stunning 27″ vertical touch screen display to send text message, email, and can also share their photos to Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest.

Open Air Style Setup

Our Selfie Booth™ is the "it" thing to add some fun to your event and make it even more memorable. Our boot has nothing that separates your guests from one another and photo sessions are much visible to others. The open air style setup of our Selfie Booth™ can be customized to suit your personality and your personal style. Multiple Functionality to Multiply your Fun

Capture your pictures on one side, and share it on the other side! In Selfie Booth™, two groups can use the Kiosk at any given time, with one group posing for pictures and other sharing them. This drastically reduces long line and waiting time. Add additional touchscreen sharing stations to accommodate huge crowds!
